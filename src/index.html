Spcace Invaders

the famous space invaders game

Play keys :

  • Moving Left : press arrow left ← or press key Q
  • Moving Right : press arrow right → or press key D
  • Shooting : press key SPACE
  • Start or Pause : press Enter or left Mouse Click

Adjust volume keys :

  • arrow up : to increase the sound
  • arrow down : to decrease the sound
  • M :to stop the sound
  • L :to restart the sound
